Sensory Art Centre

Renovating the Sensory Garden and depot buildings at Nuns Moor Park into a net-zero sanctuary.

Project Overview

Build Planet Zero is collaborating with a Community Interest Company (CIC) to transform the underused Sensory Garden and adjacent depot buildings in Nuns Moor Park (West End, Newcastle) into a net-zero Community Arts Centre.

This initiative aims to revitalise a cherished green space, delivering both environmental innovation and community benefit.

Sustainable Material Strategy

Hydraform Blocks

Fire-resistant, breathable wall insulation that regulates indoor humidity and provides thermal comfort.

Straw Bale Panels

Strong insulation properties, compostable and biodegradable. Significantly reduces energy consumption.

CLT Framework

Cross-Laminated Timber is carbon-neutral, modular, and enhances structural efficiency.

Project Goals

  • The Individual: Promote mental health & well-being.

  • The Community: Foster unity through shared creative space.

  • The Planet: Achieve net-zero emissions using renewable energy and sustainable materials.
