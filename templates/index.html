Pioneering Sustainable
Construction

Build Planet Zero is a forward-thinking construction firm based in the North East of England. We were founded to address the urgent need for carbon reduction in the built environment.

Unlike traditional builders, we approach every project with a system-thinking mindset. We don't just build structures; we create integrated, energy-efficient ecosystems that reduce operational costs and environmental impact.

Our Technical Edge

Core Specialties

Material Innovation

Specializing in SIPs with Hempcrete, Hydraform blocks, and Recycled Cork flooring to eliminate embodied carbon.

ZEM-Home Development

Flexible, scalable, and smart energy housing solutions. Modular designs like the Rowlands Gill eco-conscious residences.

Masterplanning

Zero-carbon feasibility studies and urban design for massive projects like the Shanghai Disney Neighbourhood.

Eco-Retrofitting

Transforming underused depots and existing buildings into net-zero hubs, as seen in Nuns Moor Park.

Social Integration

Guided by the 3 Es (Environment, Economy, Equity) to ensure development harmonizes with existing neighborhoods.

Self-Energy Sufficiency

Integrating Solar Arrays, Triple Glazing, and Reinforced Rubber Roofing for off-grid capable living.
